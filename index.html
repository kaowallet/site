Paranoid under the hood (￢‿￢)

Every feature exists to answer one question: HOW DO YOU KNOW? Kao never asks you to trust a server, an RPC, or a blob of hex.

#01 (`･ω･´)

Safe multisig from v0

Not bolted on later — Kao speaks Safe natively from its first release. Propose, collect signatures, and execute without ever touching a web app.

safe · native
#02 (◕‿◕)

Clear signing

No more signing mystery hex. Every transaction is decoded into plain words — what moves, where it goes, and what it's allowed to do.

human-readable calldata
#03 (๑˃ᴗ˂)ﻭ

Local simulation · revm

Before you sign, Kao runs the transaction on your machine with revm and shows you the exact balance changes. No surprises on-chain.

revm · on-device
#04 (￣ω￣)

Zero JavaScript

A native desktop app with no embedded browser, no remote code, no supply-chain roulette. The attack surface a wallet should have: small.

no webview · no remote code
#05 (✧ω✧)

Verified balances · helios

Balances come with consensus proofs via an embedded helios light client — verified against Ethereum itself, not somebody's RPC.

helios light client
#06 (/ω＼)

Encrypted local database

Your accounts, labels, and history live in an encrypted database on your disk. Nothing synced, nothing phoned home, nothing to subpoena.

encrypted at rest

Built on boring, audited things ( ‾́ ◡ ‾́ )

Kao doesn't invent cryptography or consensus. It composes the most-reviewed pieces of the Ethereum stack and wraps them in a face.

(๑˃ᴗ˂)ﻭ > revm

The Rust EVM used by reth and Foundry. Kao embeds it to replay your transaction against live state, entirely offline.

(✧ω✧) > helios

A trustless light client. Kao checks every balance and state read against Ethereum consensus proofs — RPCs become untrusted couriers.

(✿◠‿◠) > safe

The most battle-tested multisig contracts on Ethereum. Kao is a first-class Safe client from version zero.
